This is a strategy game. Click 'roll' to roll the die. Each time you roll the die, whatever you roll will be added to yoru current score. BUT if you roll a 1 then your current score is reset to 0 and you lose your turn. At any time during your turn you can click 'hold' to save your score and pass your turn to the other player. The first player to reach 100 points wins.
